Buger Recipe

Description

This recipe is for one of my favorite burger patties! They are soft and just great overall.These burgers will make you want more unless you dont like unions.

I promise you that you need to try these bugers and lets get right to how to make them!

  • Ground beef
  • 2 egss
  • 2 slices of bread
  • choped unions
  1. Put a good size of ground beef into a bowl
  2. Take you 2 slices of bread and soak them in some water and squeeze the water out we dont want them to be mush
  3. After adding them in now add the 2 eggs
  4. (opional)Chop some unions and add them in
  5. Mix them together untill they are well mixed
  6. Then cook them up and you done!
