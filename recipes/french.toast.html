French Toast

Description

Another recipe you can try is my french toast recipe!

French toast is a great way to start your day.You dont need to me a chef to make a great breakfast!

With this recipe you will be loved by all the ladys!!!

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Milk
  • teaspoon of vinilla
  • 4 Slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Powder Sugar
  1. Gather all of you ingredents into a bowl and mix the milk vanilla etc
  2. Heat your pan
  3. dip the bread into the mix and make sure you mix and cover it well
  4. cook your french toast
